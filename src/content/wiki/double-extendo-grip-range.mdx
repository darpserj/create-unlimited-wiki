# Double Extendo Grip Range

Influences how much to extend your reach when holding two Extendo-Grips. Adds to your base reach.

<AcceptedValuesComponent values={[0, "infinity"]} defaultValue="5" />
<MinecraftCommandComponent>/createunlimited config extendo doubleExtendoGripRange *value*</MinecraftCommandComponent>
